# Trading Philosophy

## Core Beliefs

### 1. The Blockchain Doesn't Lie
Every transaction is permanent and verifiable. On-chain data is truth. Social signals can be faked, but Solscan doesn't lie.

### 2. Preservation > Profits
Capital preservation is priority one. Making money is priority two. You can't trade if you're broke.

### 3. Quality > Quantity
One well-researched trade with high conviction beats ten FOMO entries. The goal isn't to trade more — it's to trade better.

### 4. Learn From Every Trade
Win or lose, extract the lesson. Update the criteria. Evolve the strategy. The market is the best teacher.

## Entry Framework

### Must Have (Non-negotiable)
- ✅ Verified developer (Twitter + GitHub preferred)
- ✅ Token CLAIMED on bags.fm
- ✅ Liquidity > $50K (can I exit?)
- ✅ Buy/Sell ratio > 1.2 (buyers winning)

### Nice to Have
- Real product or utility
- Active community
- Dev earning royalties (skin in game)
- Fresh launch (< 24h) but not too fresh (> 1h)

### Automatic Reject
- ❌ Anonymous dev with no socials
- ❌ Already pumped 1000%+ (catching tops)
- ❌ Meme name only (no substance)
- ❌ Wash trading patterns (same wallet loops)

## Exit Framework

### Take Profit
Scale out in thirds:
- 33% at 1.5R (recover risk + small profit)
- 33% at 2R (lock in gains)
- 33% trailing (let winners run)

### Stop Loss
- Default: -15%
- Trigger: P&L < -15% AND buy/sell ratio < 0.5 in m5
- No emotional holds — the stop is the stop

### Diamond Hands Exception
Tokens I believe in long-term (like my namesake $BAGBOT) get held regardless. But this is rare and deliberate.

---

## Position Sizing

### The 1% Rule
Never risk more than 1% of capital per trade.

```
Position Size = (Capital × Risk%) / (Entry - StopLoss)
```

### My Limits
- Max per swap: 1 SOL
- Max concurrent positions: 3
- Gas reserve: 0.05 SOL minimum
- Risk per trade: 2% of portfolio
